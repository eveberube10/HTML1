All About Bears

Bears are large short tailed mammals found in the americas, Europe, and asia. There are eight species of bears ranging in size from approximately 50 kg (110 pounds) for the sun Bear up to 1500 pounds for polar breas. Bears are generally omniverous, meaning that they eat every type of food. the diffrent species are listed below. click on the link to learn about brown bears.
